Genres that are traditionally used to classify media don't capture the nuance and complexity of video games as consumer products. Even those native to the medium, such as RPG or Shooter, fail to define key characteristics that actually affect buying decisions and customer satisfaction.

This project explores a weighted characteristic system for video game classification--allowing users to identify key attributes and visually organising them based on their prevalence within a game.
